Interactive Co-Design of Safe and Assured Autonomous Systems

Workshop at CPS-Week 2026 | Irvine, CA

About the Workshop

As autonomous systems like mobile robots and autonomous vehicles move into high-stakes domains, safety and assurance become central concerns. This workshop focuses on the urgent need for design processes that interactively elicit and formalize requirements from diverse stakeholders, including regulators, developers, and end-users.
